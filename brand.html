/ BRAND

Identity system

A loud, professional mark built for a forward deploy engineer who also runs AI. Black field, one hot accent, a square monogram, and a mono label. It reads the same on a business card, a slide, or a terminal.

Monogram

The JM mark. Square frame, accent edge, grain. Primary lockup on dark, reversed on light.

Palette

Ink #0a0a0b. Paper #f4f1ea. Signal #ff4d2e. Wire #2e6bff. One accent at a time, never all at once.

Ag
JetBrains Mono

Type

Archivo 800 for statements. JetBrains Mono for labels and data. Uppercase, tight tracking, no soft corners.

/ USE

How it is applied

  • Logo sits top-left on every page, always links home.
  • Accent is reserved for one active element per view: a link, a tag, a live dot.
  • Never tilt, never add gradients, never stretch the frame.
  • On light backgrounds, invert to paper field with ink mark.
