FieldConsole

Live fleet ops console

A WebSocket-driven console for managing remote site deployments. Each site in your fleet has a live status. Hit Deploy and watch it flip from deploying to healthy in real time, with a streaming log tail. The thing you leave open on the second monitor during a rollout.

Live view. Eight sites, each with its own status and Deploy control. The log tail streams as the rollout progresses. Status updates arrive over WebSocket, no refresh.

WHAT IT DOES

Fleet8 seeded sites
TransportWebSocket, live
ActionsDeploy per site
Logstreaming tail
Statehealthy / down / deploying

PRACTICAL USE

One screen shows every location during a rollout. You act on any site without a page refresh, and the whole room sees the same truth: what is green, what is still deploying, what went down.
